学舞两年，最崩溃的事就是扒舞扣细节——播放、暂停、倒退、再播放、再暂停、倒退过头了又往前拖……一个八拍能反复拖进度条二三十次。

After two years of learning dance, the most frustrating part is scrubbing through videos for details — play, pause, rewind, play again, pause again, overshoot, drag forward... one eight-count can mean dragging the progress bar 20-30 times.

作为一个程序媛，有一天扒舞扒到崩溃的时候突然想：为什么不能写个App帮我自动拆动作？然后我下班后闷头做了一个月，终于搞出来了。

As a developer who dances, one day I snapped and thought: why can't I just build an app to split moves automatically? So I spent a month after work, and finally made it happen.

上传一个舞蹈视频，AI 会自动把整支舞拆成一个个小片段，每个片段就是一个动作。然后你可以逐段循环、慢放、镜像来学，不用再自己卡进度条了。

Upload a dance video, and AI automatically splits it into individual moves. Then you can loop, slow down, or mirror each segment — no more fighting the progress bar.

一个人做App最怕闭门造车，所以想找一些真正扒舞的姐妹帮我试试，看看到底好不好用 🙏

Building alone, I worry about missing the mark. So I'm looking for real dancers to try it out and tell me what works 🙏
